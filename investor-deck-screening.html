The Problem

A century of tillage and fertilizer stripped the biology out of the soil.

  • Continuous-corn soils are biologically degraded — bacterially-skewed, fungal-depleted. (Fungal:bacterial biomass falls forest > grassland > cropland.)
  • Farmers now rent yield from inputs every year instead of owning it in the soil.
  • The "biologicals" sold to fix this are mostly single strains — and up to ~90% fail to establish.
3 / 10

The Insight

Soil is a microbiome. The industry treats it like a chemistry set.

  • Function lives in a diverse, coupled community — fungi break down tough residue, bacteria metabolize the released nutrients and fix nitrogen.
  • Degraded soil isn't missing a kingdom — it's missing the fungal half of that handoff.
  • The fix isn't a better single strain. It's restoring the partnership and feeding it — nurture, not control.
4 / 10

Why this is real, not faith

The biology is settled. The agronomy isn't. That gap is the company.

Proven (HIGH confidence)

Degraded cropland is fungal-depleted · single-strain inoculants fail · cross-kingdom labor split recurs across biomes · fungal residue builds the most stable soil carbon.

Unproven by anyone — incumbents included

That restoring fungi causally lifts yield and carbon independent of the nutrients applied. Nobody has run the clean trial.

5 / 10

The Wedge

All improvement comes from measurement. The industry can't measure soil health.

  • A blood panel works because of validated reference ranges. Soil microbiome science has none — the standard metric (F:B ratio) is method-confounded and a contested predictor.
  • Whoever builds the function-anchored soil-biology readout owns the instrument the whole field needs and can't currently buy.
6 / 10

What we do

Characterize → Prove → Scale. Disciplined and falsifiable.

  • Product: a high-biomass, functionally-coupled fungal+bacterial inoculum (Johnson-Su lineage), delivered with prebiotic context so it establishes instead of washing out.
  • Method: identify beneficial fungi by comparison, not extraction — sample degraded soil, a healthy reference soil, and our product; the missing guild is the delta.
  • Every batch fingerprinted (sequencing + function), not crafted by feel.
