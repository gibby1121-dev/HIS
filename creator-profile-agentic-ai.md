# Creator Profile: Cole Medin

* **Platform Link**: [YouTube — @ColeMedin](https://www.youtube.com/@ColeMedin) · [GitHub — coleam00](https://github.com/coleam00) · [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cole-medin-727752184/)
* **Core Value**: Master of multi-agent orchestration frameworks.
* **Key Content Formats**: 10-minute technical breakdowns, deep-dive code repositories.

## Why This Creator

Cole Medin is a Generative AI specialist and educator (200K+ YouTube subscribers) whose entire output is centered on building and orchestrating production-grade AI agents. He breaks down complex agentic concepts into practical, end-to-end builds in n8n, Python with LangGraph, and OpenAI's Agents SDK — explicitly calling out cost, latency, and reliability tradeoffs rather than stopping at demos. He is the founder of Dynamous AI.

He earns the "master of multi-agent orchestration" label through his open-source work, most notably **Archon** — an open-source harness/workflow engine for AI coding that makes agent-driven development deterministic and repeatable by defining planning, implementation, validation, code review, and PR creation as reusable workflows.

## Signature Work

* **Archon** — [coleam00/Archon](https://github.com/coleam00/Archon): "The first open-source harness builder for AI coding." A workflow engine that encodes development processes as YAML workflows with defined phases, validation gates, and artifacts — the AI fills in the intelligence at each step while the structure stays deterministic and owned by the developer.
* **AI Agents Masterclass** — [coleam00/ai-agents-masterclass](https://github.com/coleam00/ai-agents-masterclass): The companion code repository for his flagship YouTube series; every example built on the channel is published here to fork and extend.
* **Principles of Agentic Engineering** — [coleam00/ai-transformation-workshop](https://github.com/coleam00/ai-transformation-workshop): Workshop materials and a demo app covering the "AI layer" concept, the PIV loop, and 15 reusable Claude Code commands.
